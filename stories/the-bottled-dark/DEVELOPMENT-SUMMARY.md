## FRESH TROPES & ANGLES USED

Based on research, avoided these clichés:
- ❌ "Strength becomes weakness"
- ❌ "Final moral act redemption"
- ❌ "Greed corrupts"
- ❌ Standard fairy tale subversions
- ❌ Evil fairy seeking revenge
- ❌ Hero's sacrifice saves the day

Instead, using:
- ✅ **Blue-Orange Morality**: Fairy not evil, just ALIEN
- ✅ **Nothing Is Scarier**: Horror through implication and wrongness
- ✅ **Fridge Horror**: Details that become more disturbing the more you think about them
- ✅ **Ghibli Beautiful Horror**: Lovely appearance, viscerally wrong behavior
- ✅ **The Fair Folk**: Folkloric basis - beautiful, dangerous, rule-bound, incomprehensible
- ✅ **Yokai Aesthetic**: Beautiful spirits that are genuinely dangerous (Yuki-onna, Jorogumo)
- ✅ **No Redemption Arc**: Tragedy is final, pattern completes regardless
- ✅ **Love Makes Horror Worse**: Domestic tenderness corrupted

## FINAL NOTES

This story uses **researched folklore** and **historical accuracy** to create something genuinely fresh and terrifying. **No redemption arc, no escape clause, no deus ex machina**. The pattern completes. Tragedy is final. But not meaningless - their love was real, which is why the loss devastates.
