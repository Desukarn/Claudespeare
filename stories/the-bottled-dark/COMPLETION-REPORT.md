## Writing Standards Maintained

### Ghibli Horror Aesthetic ✓
- Domestic details lovingly rendered (tea, lamplight, worn quilts)
- Beauty in poverty (steam, rain, small comforts)
- Tender sibling moments throughout
- Horror in wrongness of small details
- Gentle tone masking existential terror

### Anti-Slop Compliance ✓
- "Said" used for 80%+ of dialogue tags
- Emotions shown through actions, not named directly
- ZERO banned AI words (delve, tapestry, testament, beacon)
- Natural dialogue with contractions
- Concrete sensory details
- Varied sentence structure
- NO physical emotion clichés
- Exposition broken up with dialogue/action

### Fairy Horror Rules ✓
- Speaks gently, operates on Blue-Orange Morality
- Beautiful but WRONG (subtle wrongness)
- Never angry or cruel, just OTHER
- Doesn't understand human concepts
- Patient, alien, incomprehensible
