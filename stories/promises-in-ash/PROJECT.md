## Premise

Garicus and Solaire, two boys who survive a village raid, swear to protect each other after becoming the sole survivors. They are adopted by a traveling knight and grow up together as brothers in arms. Years later, when Solaire discovers his father is alive, everything changes. The dark fantasy explores betrayal, promises, and grief as it's revealed that Solaire's father orchestrated the attack for the greater good—to prevent a darker ritual where the village planned to sacrifice children to summon a dragon.

## Core Themes

- **Betrayal and forgiveness**: Can love survive when the person who saved you is also responsible for your greatest loss?
- **The burden of truth**: Some secrets destroy us; others set us free
- **Found family vs. blood**: What defines brotherhood—shared blood or shared suffering?
- **The greater good**: When does the end justify the means?

## Setting

A dark medieval fantasy world where:
- Magic exists but is feared and outlawed in most regions
- Dragon summoning is forbidden knowledge, punishable by death
- Traveling knights serve as both law enforcement and executioners
- Villages are isolated, superstitious, and desperate

## Story Arc Overview

This novella will span approximately 15-20 years, structured in three major arcs:

1. **The Raid** (Childhood - Age 8-9): The attack, survival, oath
2. **Brotherhood** (Adolescence - Age 16-18): Training, growth, bond deepening
3. **Revelation** (Young Adulthood - Age 23-25): Discovery, confrontation, choice
