# Characters: Promises in Ash

Quick character sketches for YOLO mode - focused on essentials to get you writing.

## Main Characters

### Garicus

**Role:** Protagonist

**Core Traits:**
- Fiercely loyal, haunted by survivor's guilt
- Pragmatic and protective
- Struggles with trust outside his chosen circle

**Background:**
Garicus was 8 when his village burned. He survived by hiding in a root cellar while hearing his mother's screams above. The only other survivor was Solaire, a boy he barely knew before that night. They swore an oath to each other in the ashes: never let each other die alone. Adopted by Sir Aldric, a knight who found them in the ruins, Garicus threw himself into training with desperate intensity—if he's strong enough, no one he loves will ever burn again.

**Primary Goal:**
Protect Solaire and prove that their bond is stronger than blood, fate, or truth.

**Voice Note:**
Speaks in short, clipped sentences. Rarely wastes words. When emotional, goes completely silent.

---

### Solaire

**Role:** Protagonist (dual protagonist with Garicus)

**Core Traits:**
- Optimistic despite trauma, seeks meaning in suffering
- Curious and questioning, always pushing boundaries
- Desperate for family and belonging

**Background:**
Solaire lost everything at 8—or so he thought. Unlike Garicus, he remembers little of the raid itself; he was knocked unconscious early and woke to smoke and silence. His memories are fragmented: his mother's lullaby, the smell of bread baking, a tall figure in the flames. When Sir Aldric took them in, Solaire latched onto the idea of found family with desperate hope. Garicus became his anchor, Aldric his father figure. Years later, a chance encounter with a merchant reveals the impossible: his father lives.

**Primary Goal:**
Understand why his father abandoned him and reconcile the man he finds with the father he's imagined.

**Voice Note:**
Talks more when nervous. Asks questions constantly. Laughs to defuse tension.

---

### Sir Aldric

**Role:** Mentor / Father Figure

**Core Traits:**
- Honorable but world-weary
- Carries secrets he believes will die with him
- Loves the boys but fears they'll hate him if they learn the truth

**Background:**
A knight of the Order of the Crimson Vigil, Aldric has seen darkness most can't imagine. He arrived at the burned village on assignment—investigating rumors of forbidden rituals. What he found were two traumatized boys and ashes. He took them in knowing he was complicit in their orphaning, though he'd argued against the raid. He raised them as his own, teaching them the blade and the code, always waiting for the day they'd ask questions he couldn't answer.

**Primary Goal:**
Protect the boys from the truth for as long as possible, and prepare them to survive when it inevitably comes out.

**Voice Note:**
Formal, measured. Uses parables and lessons instead of direct answers. Rare moments of warmth quickly masked by gruffness.

---

### Theron (Solaire's Father)

**Role:** Antagonist / Tragic Figure

**Core Traits:**
- Ruthlessly utilitarian—calculates lives like numbers
- Haunted by his choice, justifies it endlessly
- Genuinely loved his son but loved "the greater good" more

**Background:**
Theron was a priest in a village that fell to darkness. The village elders, desperate and starving, turned to forbidden texts: summon a dragon, offer child sacrifices for prosperity. Theron discovered the plot too late to stop it from within. He fled, sought help from the Order of the Crimson Vigil, and led them back. The village burned. Theron thought his son died in the flames—only to discover years later, through guilt-driven inquiries, that Solaire survived. He's been watching from afar ever since, unable to approach, unwilling to stay away.

**Primary Goal:**
Convince Solaire that his actions, though monstrous, prevented something far worse—and perhaps earn forgiveness, or at least understanding.

**Voice Note:**
Speaks in justifications. Every sentence is a defense. Calls atrocities "necessary measures" and children "acceptable losses."

## Quick Reference

Characters at a glance:

- **Garicus** - Protagonist, survivor, protector
- **Solaire** - Protagonist, seeker, optimist
- **Sir Aldric** - Mentor, keeper of secrets
- **Theron** - Solaire's father, architect of the raid
- **The Burned** (minor) - Ghosts/visions that haunt Garicus

## Development Notes

This is a YOLO starter template - lightweight by design.

**As you write:**
- Add details when they emerge naturally
- Characters can surprise you
- Backstory comes when needed
- Relationships develop through scenes

**Potential additions:**
- Secondary characters from the Order
- Merchants, innkeepers, witnesses
- Surviving villagers from other burned towns
- The dragon cult's remnants

**The YOLO approach:** Discover characters through writing, not planning. These sketches give you a starting point. Let the story tell you who these people really are.
