## Development Notes

This is a YOLO starter outline - high-level structure without scene-by-scene detail.

**As you write:**
- Scenes will emerge between these beats
- Subplots may develop naturally
- The path from beat to beat can surprise you
- Some beats may shift or merge

**The YOLO approach:** These are guideposts, not prescriptions. Major story beats are mapped, but the journey between them is yours to discover. If the story wants to go somewhere unexpected, follow it. Update this outline to match what you actually write, not the other way around.

**When to update:**
- After writing major turning points
- When a subplot emerges
- If the story takes an unexpected turn
- When you discover the real theme

This outline serves you - don't let it become a constraint.
