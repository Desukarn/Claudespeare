## How It Was Built

### Task 1: Character Arc Template
Created .planning/templates/arcs.md with:
- **Starting Point**: Beliefs, flaws, emotional state, skills/abilities baseline
- **Arc Types**: Positive change, flat, negative, corruption arcs with explanations
- **Key Moments of Growth**: Structured format for 3-4+ transformative moments
  - What happens (event description)
  - What they learn (lesson/realization)
  - How they change (specific transformation)
- **Ending Point**: New beliefs, growth achieved, ending emotional state, final capabilities
- **Tracking Checklist**: Ensures proper setup, distribution, and earned transformation
- **Multiple Character Support**: Structure repeatable for all major characters
- **Examples**: Provided throughout for guidance

### Task 2: World-Building Template
Created .planning/templates/world.md with:
- **Setting**: Time period, location, scope, overview
- **World Rules**:
  - Magic/Technology/Science: System, source, limitations, costs, key rules
  - Social Structure: Governance, power structure, classes, mobility, institutions
  - Culture: Values, taboos, customs, religion, language
- **Geography**: Multiple location sections with description, significance, features, population
- **History**: Timeline of major events, backstory elements, conflicts, lost knowledge
- **Consistency Tracking**:
  - Details to Maintain: Concrete facts list
  - Established Facts: Chapter-referenced facts as story develops
  - Rules Check: Pre-scene verification checklist
- **Examples**: Detailed examples throughout demonstrating usage
